Welcome to Notessmasher 💡

Your notes deserve more than messy folders. Notessmasher is here to turn scattered study material into a clean, smart, beautiful platform — by students, for students.

📖 Our Story

I’m A*****a (IT'S A SECRET 😉), a Computer Science student who got tired of juggling notes, losing files, and searching forever for “that one PDF.” I decided to stop complaining — and start building.

What began as a small side project is now a growing space where students can upload, organize, and share study resources with clarity and style.

🎯 Our Vision

To empower students by turning clutter into clarity, and stress into structure. StudySpark isn’t just about uploading notes — it’s about sparking ideas, connection, and confidence.
